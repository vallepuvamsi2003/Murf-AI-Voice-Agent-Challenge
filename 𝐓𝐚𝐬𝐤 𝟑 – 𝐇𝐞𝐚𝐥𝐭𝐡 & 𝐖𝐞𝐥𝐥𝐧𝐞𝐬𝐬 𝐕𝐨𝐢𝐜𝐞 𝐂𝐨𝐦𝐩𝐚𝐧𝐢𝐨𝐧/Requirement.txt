### 5. How to Test (Verification Steps)

1.  **First Session:**
    * Click "Start Session".
    * The Agent will say: *"Hello! I'm your wellness companion..."*
    * Reply: *"I'm feeling a bit tired but okay."*
    * Agent asks for goals.
    * Reply: *"I want to finish my coding task and go for a walk."*
    * Agent recaps. Say: *"Yes, that's right."*
    * **Check:** Look in `backend/wellness_log.json`. You should see your data there.

2.  **Second Session (The "Memory" Test):**
    * Refresh the browser page.
    * Click "Start Session".
    * **Success Condition:** The Agent should say: *"Welcome back. Last time we spoke on [Date], you mentioned feeling **I'm feeling a bit tired but okay**. How are you today?"*
